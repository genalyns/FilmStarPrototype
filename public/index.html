What is Film Star?

FilmStar is a movie tracking website for keeping track of finished and unfinished movies, in any genre. This website specifically targets movie lovers who do not have time to be watching movies in one go. FilmStar makes it easy and sufficient for busy students and workers to enjoy their leisure time away from work or school to be stress-free when continuing their desired movie.
